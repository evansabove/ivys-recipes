---
title: "Chicken and Mushroom Stroganoff"
description: "A rich and creamy stroganoff with tender chicken and earthy mushrooms — this is a dinner guaranteed not to leave you hungry!"
date: 2026-03-30
draft: true
category: Dinner
prepTime: "10 mins"
cookTime: "25 mins"
servings: 4
tags: ["Chicken", "Comfort", "Creamy"]
ingredients:
  - group: "For the stroganoff"
    items:
      - "4 chicken thighs, boneless and skinless"
      - "1 large onion"
      - "1 medium carrot"
      - "1 stick celery"
      - "1 pack mushrooms"
      - "2 cloves garlic"
      - "1 handful parsley"
      - "1 tbsp plain flour"
      - "Chicken stock"
      - "3 tbsp creme fraiche"
  - group: "To serve"
    items:
      - "Brown rice, white rice or pasta"
      - "Flat-leaf parsley"
steps:
  - "Get a frying pan nice and hot, then sear the chicken thighs in 1 tbsp olive oil. There's no need to cook them all the way through at this stage. Remove and set aside."
  - "While that's frying, finely chop the onion, carrot and celery. Keeping the pan on a high heat, add them to the pan and cook for 5-8 minutes until softened and starting to brown. Start the seasoning now with a pinch of sea salt and black pepper. We'll add more later - and don't go mad as the stock is salty."
  - "Add the mushrooms and cook for 5-8 minutes until they release their liquid and start to brown."
  - "Finely chop the garlic and parsley stalks. Add them to the pan and cook for a minute until fragrant."
  - "Add 1 tbsp plain flour and stir to cook out."
  - "Pour in the chicken stock and bring to a simmer. Add the chicken thighs back into the pan and let them simmer gently for 20-25 minutes until cooked through and tender."
  - "Stir in the creme fraiche and bring to a gentle simmer. Taste and add more salt and pepper if needed."
  - "Chop the parsley leaves and stir them through the sauce."
  - "Remove from the heat and it is ready to serve!"
serve: "Serve with brown rice, white rice or pasta."
tips:
  - "You can use bone-in chicken thighs, but increase the cook time appropriately."
  - "I used a stainless steel frying pan for this - the chicken really browns nicely in it and gives lots of flavour. Preheat it well!"
  - "Keep the pan nice and hot in the early stages of cooking - getting a good sear on the chicken and vegetables is key to building flavour."
---
